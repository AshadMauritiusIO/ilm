Module 04: Core Curriculum

The Methodology of Dāʿwah

An Epistemic Analysis of Divine Invitation, Communicative Ethics, and Intellectual Engagement

01. The Tripartite Blueprint

Surah An-Nahl [16:125]

ٱدْعُ إِلَىٰ سَبِيلِ رَبِّكَ بِٱلْحِكْمَةِ وَٱلْمَوْعِظَةِ ٱلْحَسَنَةِ ۖ وَجَـٰدِلْهُم بِٱلَّتِى هِىَ أَحْسَنُ ۚ

Translation

"Invite to the way of your Lord with wisdom and good instruction, and argue with them in a way that is best..."

Wisdom (Al-Ḥikmah)

Analyzing listener demographics, capacity, and emotional frameworks before presenting semantic concepts.

Good Counsel (Al-Mawʿiẓah)

Articulating statements with clear, compassionate, and sincere rhetoric that connects directly to human experience.

Optimal Debate (Al-Jidāl)

Maintaining flawless intellectual composure and strict behavioral ethics if conversations transform into debates.

02. Commanded Softness to Power

Surah Taha [20:44]

فَقُولَا لَهُۥ قَوْلًۭا لَّيِّنًۭا لَّعَلَّهُۥ يَتَذَكَّرُ أَوْ يَخْشَىٰ

Translation

"And speak to him with gentle speech that perhaps he may remember or fear [God]."

Structural Lesson: Even when confronting absolute political tyranny (Pharaoh), the initial communicative baseline commanded by God is structural gentleness (Qawlan Layyina), rejecting systemic aggression or upfront emotional hostility.

03. Autonomy of Intellect and Conscience

Surah Al-Baqarah [2:256] & Al-Ghashiyah [88:21-22]

لَآ إِكْرَاهَ فِى ٱلدِّينِ ۖ

"There is no compulsion in religion..."

فَذَكِّرْ إِنَّمَآ أَنتَ مُذَكِّرٌۭ • لَّسْتَ عَلَيْهِم بِمُصَيْطِرٍ

"So remind, [O Muhammad]; you are only a reminder. You are not over them a controller."

The absolute core metric of Quranic dawah is message transmission transparency (Al-Balāgh). True psychological faith cannot operate via transactional manipulation or force; individual choice must follow clean, unimpeded intellectual and spiritual contemplation.
